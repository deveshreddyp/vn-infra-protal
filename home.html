About This Application

This is an AI-powered portal designed to streamline the hiring process for VN Infra Reyality.

  • Recruiter Portal: Allows recruiters to automatically scan and score resumes against a job description. It also lists all submitted applications, highlighting shortlisted candidates.
  • Candidate Checker: A tool for candidates to check how well their resume matches an open position before they apply.
  • Apply for a Job: A simple form that automatically scans the applicant's resume, assigns a match score, and notifies the hiring team.

This system saves time and helps identify qualified candidates more efficiently.
